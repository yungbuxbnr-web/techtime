
# Monthly Absence Reset - User Guide

## What Is It?

The app now automatically resets your absence records at the beginning of each new month. This means you start fresh every month without having to manually clear your absence hours.

## How It Works

### Automatic Reset

When you open the app on the first day of a new month (or any day in a new month if you haven't opened it yet), the app will:

1. Detect that a new month has started
2. Automatically reset your absence hours to 0
3. Show you a friendly notification confirming the reset

### What You'll See

When the reset happens, you'll see a notification like this:


This lets you know that:
- Your absence hours have been reset for the new month
- The previous month's data has been cleared
- You can start logging absence for the new month

## Using the Absence Logger

### Logging Absence

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to **üèñÔ∏è Absence Logger**
3. Select:
   - **Number of Absent Days** (1-31 days)
   - **Absence Type** (Half Day or Full Day)
   - **Deduction Type** (Monthly Target or Available Hours)
4. Review the calculation preview
5. Tap **‚úÖ Log Absence & Update Hours**

### Absence Types

**Half Day**
- Deducts 4.25 hours per day
- Use for partial day absences

**Full Day**
- Deducts 8.5 hours per day
- Use for full day absences

### Deduction Types

**Monthly Target Hours**
- Reduces your monthly target hours permanently
- Affects the "Monthly Target" progress circle
- Use when you want to adjust your monthly goal

**Total Available Hours**
- Reduces hours used in efficiency calculations
- Affects the "Efficiency" circle
- Use when tracking time off without changing your target

## Monthly Reset Behavior

### What Happens Each Month

**Last Day of Month:**
- Your absence hours are still tracked
- All calculations include your logged absences

**First Day of New Month:**
- When you open the app, absence hours reset to 0
- You see a notification confirming the reset
- You can immediately start logging absence for the new month

### What Doesn't Reset

The monthly reset only affects absence hours. These remain unchanged:
- Your job records
- Your monthly target hours setting
- Your PIN and security settings
- Your theme preference
- All other app settings

## Viewing Your Absence Impact

### Dashboard

The Dashboard shows how absence affects your metrics:

**Monthly Target Circle**
- Shows progress toward your target hours
- If you deducted absence from monthly target, the target is lower

**Efficiency Circle**
- Shows your efficiency percentage
- If you deducted absence from available hours, efficiency is calculated accordingly

**Monthly Breakdown Card**
- Shows total available hours (includes absence deductions)
- Shows efficiency percentage

### Settings

In Settings, you can see:
- Current month's total absence hours
- Calculation preview before logging absence
- Impact on monthly target or available hours

## Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Log Absence Promptly**: Log your absence as soon as you know about it
2. **Check Preview**: Always review the calculation preview before confirming
3. **Choose Correct Type**: Use Monthly Target for planned time off, Available Hours for unexpected absences
4. **Monthly Review**: At the end of each month, review your absence impact on efficiency

### Common Questions

**Q: What if I forget to log absence?**
A: You can log absence at any time during the month. The calculations will update immediately.

**Q: Can I see previous months' absence?**
A: Currently, only the current month's absence is tracked. Previous months' data is not stored.

**Q: What if I log absence by mistake?**
A: There's no undo button, but you can adjust your monthly target hours manually in Settings to compensate.

**Q: Does absence affect my job records?**
A: No, absence only affects your target hours and efficiency calculations. Your job records remain unchanged.

**Q: What happens if I don't open the app for a whole month?**
A: When you next open the app, it will detect the new month and reset automatically, regardless of how much time has passed.

## Troubleshooting

### Absence Hours Not Resetting

If your absence hours don't reset at the start of a new month:

1. Make sure your device date is correct
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Navigate to Dashboard (reset check happens there)
4. Check Settings to verify absence hours

### Notification Not Appearing

The reset notification only appears on the Dashboard screen. If you don't see it:

1. Navigate to Dashboard
2. Check if absence hours in Settings are 0
3. If they're 0, the reset happened successfully

### Incorrect Calculations

If efficiency or target calculations seem wrong:

1. Check Settings ‚Üí Absence Logger
2. Verify your current month's absence hours
3. Ensure you're using the correct deduction type
4. Review the calculation preview before logging
